User Manual

A detailed guide on how to use the Binhinav system, broken down by user role.

Admin Portal: The Command Center

The Admin Portal provides full control over the venue's digital infrastructure. The typical workflow involves setting up the venue's structure first, then managing its day-to-day operations.

Initial Venue Setup

Before adding individual stores, the foundational elements of your venue must be configured. This is typically done only once or when major renovations occur.

  1. 1. Manage Floor Plans: Navigate to the "Floor Plans" section. Here you will upload the 2D map images for each level of your venue (e.g., "Ground Floor", "Level 2"). This is the canvas on which everything else is placed.
  2. 2. Create Categories: Go to the "Categories" section to define the types of places in your venue (e.g., "Food & Beverage", "Apparel"). Select a distinct icon for each to help visitors with filtering.

Managing Places and Merchants

This is the core operational workflow for adding and managing tenant spaces.

  1. Navigate to "Places" and click "Add New Place".
  2. Step 1 - Enter Details: Provide a name, select the correct Floor Plan, and then choose how to handle the merchant account:
    • Assign Existing Merchant: Choose from a list of created merchants who are not yet assigned to a place.
    • Create New Merchant: Select this to create a new merchant account (name, username, password) that will be automatically linked to this new place.
    • Leave Unassigned: Create the place now and assign a merchant later.
  3. Step 2 - Set Location: After submitting details, you will be prompted to click on the floor plan map to place a pin, precisely marking the store's location.
  4. Editing: You can edit any place's details or location at any time from the "Places" page.

Day-to-Day Operations

  • Kiosks: In the "Kiosks" section, create entries for your physical devices. A unique **Pairing Key** is generated for each, which is used for the one-time setup on the physical kiosk.
  • Ads: Manage the promotional images and videos that appear on the kiosk's idle screen. You can activate/deactivate ads and reorder them via drag-and-drop.
  • Dashboard & Recent Changes: Regularly check the Dashboard for valuable analytics on visitor searches and monitor the "Recent Changes" page to see what updates merchants have made to their listings.

Merchant Portal: Your Digital Storefront

The Merchant Portal is a simple yet powerful tool that gives you direct control over how your business is represented in the directory.

Updating Your Store Information

After logging in, you will land on the "Store Information" page. From here, you can manage every aspect of your public listing:

  • Logo & Cover Photo: Hover over the images and click the camera icon to upload new files. A square logo and a wide (16:9) cover photo are recommended.
  • Store Details: Click the "Edit" button in the main header to change your store's name or assign it to a category.
  • About & Business Hours: Use the "Edit" buttons on the corresponding cards to update your store's description and opening times. This ensures visitors always have the most accurate information.

Kiosk Application: The Visitor Experience

The kiosk is the interactive heart of Binhinav, designed for simplicity and ease of use.

For the Administrator: One-Time Setup

When a new kiosk device is turned on for the first time, it will be in "Provisioning Mode".

  1. In the Admin Portal, create a new Kiosk entry to generate a 6-character Pairing Key.
  2. Enter this key on the kiosk's touchscreen.
  3. Once provisioned, the kiosk is permanently linked to your venue and will automatically launch the main application on startup.

For the Visitor: Finding Your Way

A visitor's journey is designed to be intuitive:

  • Search & Discover: Use the prominent search bar to find a specific store, or tap on a Category button to browse all related locations, which will be highlighted on the map.
  • Explore the Map: Pan and zoom the 2D map with familiar touch gestures. Tap on any store's pin to open its details.
  • Get Directions: When a place is selected from a search or the map, the system will show its details. Clicking "Show on Map" will intelligently zoom and position the map to show the location relative to the kiosk.
  • Idle Mode: If the kiosk is not used for 60 seconds, a beautiful, full-screen display of advertisements will appear. A simple tap anywhere on the screen instantly returns to the main map interface.
