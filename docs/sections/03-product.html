Product Overview

binhinav: A Cohesive Digital Ecosystem

Binhinav is more than just a map; it's a fully-integrated ecosystem designed to connect venue administrators, merchants, and visitors. Each component works in harmony to create a seamless, informative, and manageable indoor navigation experience.

The Interactive Kiosk

The public face of Binhinav. Our sleek, user-friendly kiosks transform the visitor experience from confusing to effortless.

  • Intuitive Search: Visitors can quickly find stores or categories with a powerful, responsive search.
  • Smart Map Navigation: The map automatically zooms and pans to frame the route from the kiosk to the selected destination.
  • Engaging Promotions: An integrated ad system captures attention during idle times, driving traffic to tenants.

The Admin Portal

The central command center, giving administrators complete control over the entire Binhinav environment from any web browser.

  • Total Venue Management: Easily upload floor plans, manage all places, and provision new kiosks.
  • Merchant & Ad Oversight: Create and assign merchant accounts and manage the ad rotation system.
  • Data-Driven Insights: Access analytics on search trends to make informed business decisions.

The Merchant Portal

Empowering your tenants with the autonomy to manage their own digital storefront information simply and securely.

  • Real-Time Updates: Merchants can update their store details, logos, and business hours instantly.
  • Simple & Secure: An easy-to-use interface ensures tenants can manage their presence without needing technical skill.
  • Increased Accuracy: Reduces administrative workload and ensures directory information is always up-to-date.
